KRSLOT (한글·섬 슬롯) Privacy Policy

개인정보 처리방침 · Last updated: June 8, 2026

KRSLOT (“we”, “the App”) is a third-party slot game on Pi Network operated by the app developer. This policy explains what information the App collects when you use it through Pi Browser.

1. Information from Pi Network

When you log in with Pi, you consent to share the following with this App (via Pi SDK):

  • Pi username — displayed in the game UI
  • Pi UID — used to identify your game balance and sessions
  • Access token — sent to our backend only for verification and π payments
  • Payments scope — only when you choose π deposit (not at login)

Pi Network does not save third-party app data unrelated to Pi on your behalf. We do not request or collect location, contacts, photos, or other device data.

2. Information we process

  • Game balance and bet history (stored locally in your browser: localStorage / sessionStorage)
  • π deposit, payout, and session settlement records (processed via our backend API)
  • Payment metadata (amounts, memos, transaction IDs) required for Pi payments

3. How we use data

  • Authenticate you with Pi Platform API (/me verification)
  • Run the game economy (KR balance, π deposit / withdraw / prizes)
  • Prevent abuse (rate limiting on the server)

4. Sharing and storage

  • Access tokens are verified on our server (han-xe9x.onrender.com) and are not published publicly.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Pi payment operations use Pi Network’s official APIs and app wallet.

5. Data retention

Browser session data is cleared when you close the session or clear site data. Server logs may retain anonymized technical records for a limited time for security.

6. Your choices

  • You may decline Pi login and not use π features (demo mode in non-Pi browsers).
  • You may revoke consent by not using the App; clear browser storage to remove local data.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under the age required by local law for cryptocurrency use.
